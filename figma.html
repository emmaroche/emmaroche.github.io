Memory Lane Event Planning Website

As part of my User Experience Design Module in my Master's, I invented a fictional company called Memory Lane Event Planning, that specialises in upscale event planning. The goal of Memory Lane Event Planning, is to craft unforgettable experiences for various occasions like weddings, milestone birthdays, and anniversaries.

The purpose of the website design is to create a professional, intuitive and user-friendly platform for people seeking upscale event planning services. The website aims to showcase the company's expertise and portfolio and facilitate communication between clients and the event planning team.

Careers Guidance App

The Careers Guidance App is an iOS mobile application I created for my Final Year Project for my honours degree. The images showcased on the right portray the prototype design of the app that I created prior to commencing its development in Xcode.

The Careers Guidance App is designed to serve as a career guidance assistant for people in search of a third level undergraduate course in the Republic of Ireland. In order to help provide the user with the necessary information and support during their course search, the app includes a course matching questionnaire. Similar to a personality test, this questionnaire asks the user questions related to their interests and skills, both personal and academic. Once the user completes the questionnaire, course suggestions are generated that best match to their personality traits, skills and interests. The suggested courses are analysed based on the responses the user provided throughout the questionnaire.

Travel Planner App

The Travel Planner App is a mobile application I initially designed as a personal project. I worked on it further as part of my Software Engineering module in the third year of my honours degree. The images showcased on the right portray the prototype design of the app that I created.

The Travel Planner App is a digital wallet/filing system. You can add the location you are going to for your holidays and how long you plan on going for. Your boarding pass, passport copy, check-in details for accommodation, any transport tickets, and any extra documentation can be uploaded and kept within this app. You can also add all the attractions you plan to see on your holiday, and it will also give you recommendations of more attractions in the area you are going to that you can visit. The app can also provide directions for these attractions and real-time updates (can be notifications based on the itinerary) i.e., Museum is now closed, flight delays, weather warnings, etc.
